1. Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy for Karkai Nandre for UPSC

Last updated: 03-01-2026

1. Introduction Welcome to Karkai Nandre for UPSC ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect, We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our educational services:

Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, phone number, email address and location

Usage Data: We track your learning progress, including quiz scores, lesson completion status, revision schedules, and "weak/strong" topic analysis. This data is stored to provide you with personalized performance dashboards.

Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers, to ensure app compatibility and push notification functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information We use your data for the following purposes:

To create and manage your user account.

To track your academic progress and generate performance analytics.

To schedule and send daily revision reminders (via local notifications).

To sync your progress across devices using cloud storage.

4. Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services which may collect or process your data:

Google Firebase: Used for authentication (logging in), database storage (Firestore), and analytics.

Expo: Used to facilitate push notifications and over-the-air updates.

5. Data Security We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at [Contact Email] or using the "Delete Account" feature within the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at shabharivasan.rc@karkainandre.com
