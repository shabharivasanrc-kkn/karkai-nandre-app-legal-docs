# Privacy Policy
**Privacy Policy for Karkai Nandre for UPSC**

**Last updated:** January 03, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Karkai Nandre for UPSC** ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our educational services:

* **Personal Information:** When you register, we may collect personally identifiable information, including your:
    * Full Name
    * Phone Number
    * Email Address
    * Location Data
* **Usage Data:** We track your learning progress to personalize your experience. This includes quiz scores, lesson completion status, revision schedules, and analysis of "weak" or "strong" topics.
* **Device Information:** We may collect specific information about your mobile device, such as the device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers. This helps us ensure app compatibility and manage push notifications.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for the following specific purposes:

* To create and manage your user account.
* To track your academic progress and generate personalized performance dashboards.
* To schedule and send daily revision reminders (via local notifications).
* To synchronize your progress across multiple devices using cloud storage.
* To improve the functionality and content of the application based on user usage patterns.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may employ third-party companies and services to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf. We use the following providers:

* **Google Firebase:** Used for secure authentication (logging in), cloud database storage (Firestore), and analytics to understand app usage.
* **Expo:** Used to facilitate push notifications and deliver over-the-air updates to keep your app running smoothly.

## 5. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
We retain your data only for as long as your account is active to ensure continuity of your learning progress.

* **Your Rights:** You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
* **How to Delete:** You can use the **"Delete Account"** feature located within the app settings, or contact us directly at the email provided below.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
